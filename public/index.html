How it works

01

Copy the link

Open the post in the Instagram app or site, tap the three dots, and choose "Copy link."

02

Paste & fetch

Drop it in the box above. We resolve the original media file directly — nothing is re-encoded.

03

Download

Each photo or video in the post gets its own download button, full resolution.

Reel Grab only works on content that's already public. It doesn't store links, files, or account data — every request is resolved live and discarded. Respect creators: downloaded media is still their copyright.
